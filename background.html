Professional Background

Academic History

  • 1995-1998: BE (Environmental) Honours 1. The University of Newcastle.
  • 2000-2003: PhD (with Distinction). Civil, Environmental and Mining Engineering, The University of Western Australia.
  • 2003-2006: Research Fellow. Department of Mathematics and Statistics, The University of Melbourne.
  • 2006-2009: Australian Research Council Postdoctoral Fellow. Department of Mathematics and Statistics, The University of Melbourne.
  • 2010-2011: Lecturer. Queensland University of Technology.
  • 2011-2013: Senior Lecturer. Queensland University of Technology.
  • 2013-2014: Associate Professor. Queensland University of Technology.
  • 2014-2018: Australian Research Council Future Fellow. Queensland University of Technology.
  • 2014-present: Professor. Queensland University of Technology.
